✦ Life is Strange ✦

Life is Strange is an episodic narrative adventure game developed by the French studio Dontnod Entertainment and published by Square Enix. Originally released in 2015, it redefined the modern graphic adventure genre. The game is available on a wide variety of platforms, including PC, PlayStation, Xbox, iOS, and Android, as well as the Nintendo Switch via the Remastered Collection.

The game has achieved phenomenal critical and commercial success, surpassing 20 million players worldwide since its launch. It has won over 75 "Game of the Year" awards and honors, including the prestigious BAFTA Games Award for Best Story. This success transformed the title into a major franchise, spawning several sequels and prequels, and solidifying its place as a cult classic in contemporary gaming culture.

The strength of Life is Strange lies in its perfect balance between supernatural themes and deeply grounded human emotions. Its unique aesthetic, featuring hand-painted textures and a memorable indie-folk soundtrack, creates an immediate sense of nostalgia. The game resonated particularly well with a diverse audience thanks to its authentic portrayal of adolescence, friendship, and difficult moral dilemmas, where every small action can lead to unpredictable long-term consequences.

The story takes place in October 2013 in the fictional town of Arcadia Bay, Oregon. Players take on the role of Max Caulfield, an 18-year-old photography student who suddenly discovers she has the power to rewind time. After saving her childhood best friend, Chloe Price, from a fatal accident, Max becomes entangled in a dark investigation surrounding the mysterious disappearance of another student, Rachel Amber. While uncovering the town's buried secrets and navigating the complexities of social life at Blackwell Academy, Max must also confront an apocalyptic vision: a massive storm threatening to destroy the entire bay.
