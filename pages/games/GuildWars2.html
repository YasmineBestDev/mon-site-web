✦ GUILD WARS 2 ✦

Guild Wars 2 is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed by ArenaNet and published by NCSoft. It was released in August 2012 as the sequel to the original Guild Wars game. Set in the fantasy world of Tyria, Guild Wars 2 offers players a dynamic and immersive experience with a focus on player choice and exploration.

In Guild Wars 2, players can choose from five different races: Humans, Charr, Norn, Asura, and Sylvari. Each race has its own unique starting area and storyline. Players can also select from eight different professions (classes), including Warrior, Ranger, Elementalist, and Necromancer, each with its own playstyle and abilities. Its innovative combat system allows players to actively dodge attacks and use a variety of skills based on their weapon choices.
