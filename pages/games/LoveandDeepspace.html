✦ LOVE AND DEEPSPACE ✦

Love and Deepspace is a Chinese otome game developed by Papergames, also called Infold Games. It is the first 3D otome game ever created, available only on mobile. It was released on Android and iOS on 18 January 2024. This game also features a gacha system where players can obtain different memories of their favorite love interests by spending in-game currency or real money. It is mainly a single-player game, although it is possible to add friends.

Love and Deepspace has enjoyed mind-blowing success and popularity in China, given that 60% of the game revenue is provided by chinese players, with a total of 13.64 million downloads. A year and a half after its release, the otome game generated more than 7.5 million dollars, and it already topped the rankings multiple times in the Gacha Revenue Monthly Reports. In fact, it is currently topping the October 2025 ranking with a total profit of 57,750,000 dollars.

Its success is mainly due to its immersive gameplay, combining sci-fi and romance, as well as its impressive 3D graphics and animations. It can also be explained by the fact that it is one game among the very few that targets a female audience in the gaming market, which was looked down upon at first, mainly because it is thought that women don't play and spend money on games. Regardless, Love and Deepspace proved the opposite with its huge fame, leading to growing consideration of the weight of the female audience in the gaming industry.

The player takes on the role of a female protagonist that can be customized at the beginning of the game. In the midst of a futuristic world, set in 2048, 14 years after the Chronorift Catastrophe, we follow the story of the protagonist who resides in Linkon City and who is a Deepspace Hunter. She is acquainted with Xavier, Rafayel, Zayne, Sylus and Caleb, who are the five main love interests of the game, where the player freely chooses with whom they want to share unique moments. Along with them, she unravels the mysteries of her existence, her connection with Deepspace, while spending heartfelt moments with the love interests.

✦ CHARACTERS ✦

"I've lived in a never-ending winter long enough. If you come closer, then..."

ZAYNE

CARDIAC SURGEON

CARDIAC SURGEON

Zayne can typically be found working at Akso Hospital. He is known for his cold and strict demeanor as well as his dedication to his work. He is a highly-awarded Cardiac Surgeon, best known for his research in Congenital Heart Abnormalities in neonates. As the youngest recipient of the Starcatcher Award and the Linde Award, Zayne has contributed to lowering the prevalence of congenital heart defects in newborns with his discovery that Evol genes affect the mutation rate of cells during heart development.

"My light only shines over you."

XAVIER

DEEPSPACE HUNTER

Recently, many citizens have been talking about a mysterious hunter who rescued them from Wanderers. With his combat prowess, he took down very dangerous Wanderers and disappeared in the blink of an eye, as if pursuing something greater. We reached out to the witnesses. From their accounts, it seems their mysterious savior is Xavier, a Deepspace Hunter. Xavier is able to fight monsters called Wanderers with the Player. His Evol type is Light, his primary weapon is a sword, and he often performs light-related sword combo attacks with the Player.

"Do you really think an inferno beneath the waves can't be turbulent ?"

RAFAYEL

ARTIST

As an artist, Rafayel appreciates fine art and beauty. His art style is like an inferno— passionate and fiery, always leaving a profound impression on people. Most of his paintings are centered on Lemuria, an ancient civilization conceived in the oceans. Rafayel is Lemurian, one of the last members of the ancient race, and under specific circumstances has been shown to have scales and a tail. He lives and paints in Mo Art Studio situated on an island in Whitesand Bay, and hates cats.

"Don't you see? This is what your heart truly desires."

SYLUS

ONYCHINUS'S LEADER

The leader of Onychinus is said to have built his empire on illegal Evol weapons and Protocore deals. He's the most influential, dominant figure in the N109 Zone. Sylus is confident and direct. He rarely perceives anyone as a real threat because he is usually the most powerful person in the room. Even in dangerous situations, he remains calm and self-assured. Sylus hates being bored and appears to be a bit of a thrill seeker; preferring to use fast motorcycles, jump out of planes, and use explosives to deal with problems on multiple occasions.

"Gravity starts and ends with you."

CALEB

Farspace Fleet's Colonel

Player's childhood friend. He works as a fighter pilot for the Deepspace Aviation Administration. Ever since he went to Aerospace Academy in Skyhaven, he and player don't often see each other. While he loves teasing the player, he's actually quite a reliable person who the player trusts. It is revealed he now works for the Farspace Fleet, serving as its Colonel. He has more secrets than ever before, which causes a rift between him and the player, but he is still determined to do whatever it takes to protect her.
