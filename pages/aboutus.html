WHO ARE WE ?

We are a small team of four that is united by a precise goal : sharing our love for video games. We believe games are a way to create and gather communities for those wishing to have companionship. Thanks to our coding skills, we made this project possible and we are now eager to see you taking a part of it by playing one or several of the games mentionned previously. Curious about us ?
